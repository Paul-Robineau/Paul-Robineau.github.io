Resume

  • R&D Engineer


    My engineer degree in Materials: Technology & Economy from the University of Technology of Troyes (UTT) allowed me to work in R&D during 2 Internships and now in the frame of my PhD Thesis. My first professionnal experiences, in Industrial Research at CEA Tech, gave me the skills to be autonomous on many areas and to direct my research with the short and long term technical feasability always in mind.


    From theorethical knowledge of Structural properties of Matter and Light-Matter interactions, to practical skills in Life-Cycle Assessment and Eco-conception, Materials for Energy or for Optoelectronics, adding proficient use of varied software tools (Microsoft Office, Matlab, Igor Pro, Bash, Adobe Creative Suite), and critical insights from Sociology of Organizations, History & Philosphy of Sciences, I have the profile of a reflexive engineer with systemic and interdisciplinary mindset.

  • Nanosciences Researcher


    A master in Optics and Nanotechnology allowed me to have a general overview on Nanosciences, from theorethical frameworks to current state of applications in different areas. Following this by a PhD in the area of Nano-bio-photonics let me develop specific competencies at the interface of Nanosciences, Biology, and Optics.


    My interest in Social Sciences and Humanities led me to gain knowledge on the varied considerations on the intertwined relations tensions between the research field and the civil, industrial, and political ones.

  • Physics & Ecology Teacher


    Beside my research activities, I have the pleasure to oversee pratical works in physics & chemistry as a teacher assistant since 2020 at the University of Technology of Troyes (UTT).


    I also designed and am responsible to teach a section on abiotic resources inside a general course on modeling of Human-Systems-Nature interactions. We analyze it from A to Z, looking at current consumption & production, and their future – using for that a sociotechnical perspective informed by social & environmental sciences.

  • Project Manager


    There is very few research that does not need collaboration and work organization. From group projects during courses at the university, to associative events or first-aid service, my skills in project & team organization have been developped in varied contexts.


    These experiences gave me the opportunity to create & conduct planning, to work with and sometimes manage teams from 3 to 15 people. On the software side, I master several tools like Slack/Discord/Miro, GitHub, or Trello.

  • Arts Enthousiast


    The collective effort of putting together art is one of my greatest pleasures. Whatever we are talking, acting, directing, or writing, it is something I'm not sure I can ever let go.


    After, during my university's years, writing, directing and editing a short film; involving myself in the Ch'UTT de vers association for 4 plays as an actor (of which 1 as co-writer) and 2 as director; my PhD years are the occasion to be engaged in a musical project as both co-director and actor - project paused thanks to Covid19 though.
